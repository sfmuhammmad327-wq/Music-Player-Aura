## 🛠️ User Instructions

### 1. Adding Music & Lyrics
* **Drag & Drop:** Simply drag your audio files (`.mp3`, `.wav`, `.flac`, `.ogg`, `.m4a`) combined with matching named lyrics files (`.lrc`) onto the upload zone box.
* **Manual Browse:** Click **Browse Files** to navigate to files on your local drive.

### 2. Attaching Lyrics
* To sync lyrics effortlessly, ensure your `.lrc` text file matches the exact file name format of your audio track (e.g., `SongName.mp3` and `SongName.lrc`). 
* Press **B** or click the **Microphone Lines** icon in the footer deck to switch over to full **Lyrics Focus Mode**.

### 3. Equalizer Tuning & Presets
* Manipulate individual slider tracks inside the **Studio EQ** panel to custom-adjust bass response, clean mid-tones, or clear highs. Click **Reset** to return to linear reference.

Master the app using these immediate hotkey assignments:

| Key Mapping | Control Action |
| :--- | :--- |
